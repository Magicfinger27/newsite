It’s our mission at Tomrain Design to provide client focused service through our responsible practice of Architecture. Our tradition of dedication, professionalism and outstanding customer service is a testament to that mission as we strive each day for excellence in bringing our valued clients’ ideas to life.

So far, we have been able to demonstrate through our projects an ability to create interesting places conscious of materiality, light and spatial experience.

Architecture Design

As architects we are passionate about the role of design to improve the quality of life. We design places for all of life's activities. So far, we have been able to demonstrate through our projects an ability to capture experience and space.

Interior design

As interior architects we see each client and project as a unique challenge to us and we ensure the resulting environments are exceptional and appropriate. Our objective is to deliver projects that surpass our client?s aspirations.

Exterior Design

We see design and build as a creative discipline. We don't copy/paste; our solutions are developed through a continual process of questioning. By doing so we protect ourselves from complacency and approach every project attentively and inquisitively.

Autocad Services

We bring visionary places, spaces and ideas to life. Because without context, vision is just a dream. We love to give shape, context and reality to the most inspiring projects.

Project Management

We work with our clients to develop a project plan that defines project objectives, scope, timeline, resource requirements, and potential risks and challenges.our goal is to ensure that the project is completed successfully, meeting the project objectives and delivering the desired outcomes while keeping the project on track within budget and on schedule.
